Avery's Guide to NYC - Food



Lilia




Lilia is one of my favorite restaurants throughout New York City. It is a modern Italian-American restaurant, locating in the blooming Williamsburg. The pastas are delicious, and this restaurant includes notable foods, including green leek butter as part of a dish, and candied oranges as part of a choclate ice cream dessert. Both are among my favorites.




Zuckers




Zuckers is undoubtedly one of the top bagel stops across all of the city. It offers a rich selection of fishes, including smoking salmon, spreads, whitefish, etc. (with capers on them!). They also offer amazing deserts, including blondies, brownies, cookies, and more.
